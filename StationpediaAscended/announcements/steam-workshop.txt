[h2]✨ Features[/h2]

[h3]📊 Enhanced Tooltips[/h3]
[list]
[*] Hover over any item to see detailed information
[*] Operating temperatures, pressure ranges, and power requirements
[*] Slot configurations and hash values for IC programming
[/list]

[h3]⚙️ Operational Details[/h3]
[list]
[*] Critical operational parameters at a glance
[*] Temperature and pressure operating ranges
[*] Power consumption and production values
[/list]

[h3]🐛 Bug Fixes[/h3]
[list]
[*] Fixes display issues with certain vanilla entries
[*] Corrects formatting problems in the original Stationpedia
[/list]

[h3]🎨 Custom Branding[/h3]
[list]
[*] Customize the Stationpedia icon
[*] Add your own item descriptions via descriptions.json
[/list]

[hr][/hr]

[h2]📥 Installation[/h2]

Simply subscribe to this mod! It will be installed automatically via StationeersLaunchPad (SLP).

[b]Manual Installation:[/b]
[olist]
[*] Ensure BepInEx is installed
[*] Copy [b]StationpediaAscended.dll[/b] to your [b]BepInEx/scripts/[/b] folder
[*] Launch the game!
[/olist]

[hr][/hr]

[h2]⚙️ Configuration[/h2]

[code]
{
  "ItemKitWallLight": "A basic wall-mounted light fixture. Provides illumination for indoor spaces.",
  "ItemKitSolarPanel": "Converts sunlight into electrical power. Most efficient when facing the sun."
}
[/code]
