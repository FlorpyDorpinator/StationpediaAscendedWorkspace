# Release Posts for Stationpedia Ascended v0.8.0

---

## Reddit Post (r/Stationeers)

### Title:
**[MOD] Stationpedia Ascended v0.8.0 - In-Game Notepad, Survival Manual, Video Support & More!**

### Body:

Hey Stationeers!

I'm excited to release **Stationpedia Ascended v0.4.0** - a major update that transforms Stationpedia into a comprehensive knowledge hub with an integrated note-taking system.

## 📝 Station Notepad (NEW!)
Take notes directly in-game! Press **F2** to open your personal notepad.

- **Folders & Organization** - Create folders to organize your notes
- **Global & Per-Save Notes** - Some notes follow you everywhere, others are save-specific
- **Rich Text Formatting** - Bold, italic, colors, headers, bullet points
- **Link to Stationpedia** - Type `{LINK:StructureArcFurnace;Arc Furnace}` to create clickable links that open Stationpedia pages!

## 📚 New Guides & Content

**Survival Manual** - A complete beginner's guide covering:
- First 5 minutes survival
- Suit management
- Building your first shelter
- Power systems basics
- Atmosphere control

**Game Mechanics** (with data from game code):
- **Complete Smelting Guide** - All furnace types, recipes, temperatures, alloys, Ice-Only recipes, Gaseous Fuel recipes
- **Species Survival Guide** - Human, Zrilian, and Robot requirements pulled directly from the source code

**Additional Guides:**
- Power Systems
- Airlock Systems (Basic & Advanced)
- Air Conditioning (including the "your starter kit is a bomb" warning!)
- Daylight Sensor

## 🎬 Media Support
- Embed **videos** directly in Stationpedia pages
- Inline **images** for visual guides
- Clickable **YouTube links**

## 🎨 Other Features
- **Nested Table of Contents** - Multi-level TOC with smooth scrolling
- **Vanilla/Ascended Mode** - Click the "Stationpedia" header to toggle styling (easter egg!)
- **Enhanced Tooltips** - Hover over logic types, slots, memory for detailed explanations

## Installation
Requires **BepInEx** with **StationeersLaunchPad** plugin.

**Steam Workshop:** https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3634225688
